01 Executive Summary

SMBs don't need another dashboard. They need decisions. This plan outlines a solo-operated, productized AI Analytics service that delivers a weekly "Monday Memo" with 3 actionable decisions, not charts.

Thesis:

By combining modern ELT (n8n + Airbyte + BigQuery) with an AI analysis layer (GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, LangChain), one operator in the Philippines can deliver the output of a 3-person US analytics team at 1/5th the price, with 80%+ gross margins.

  • Who: Philippines e-commerce (Shopify, Lazada, Shopee, TikTok Shop) and US/AU/NZ SMBs ($1M-$20M revenue) with messy data.
  • What: Three fixed-price tiers ($2,500 / $4,500 / $7,500) delivering insights, forecasting, and AI agents.
  • How: Productized delivery via Slack + Loom + Notion. 90% automated data pipeline, 10% expert review.
  • Goal: Reach $25,000 MRR by Month 9 with 5 clients (mix of Growth + Scale tiers).

03 Problem & Value Proposition

The Problem

  • ×Insights, not dashboards: Founders spend 5 hours/week looking at GA4, Shopify, Meta Ads and still ask "so what do I do?"
  • ×AI misuse: Businesses dump CSVs into ChatGPT and get hallucinations. No data pipeline, no context, no governance.
  • ×Hiring doesn't work: Good analysts cost ₱80-120k/month in PH, $90k+/yr in US. They quit, need management, and don't understand business.

Value Proposition

  • ✓The Monday Memo: Every Monday 8am, 3 decisions in plain English. "Pause Ad Set B, reallocate $450 to C. Expected +₱67k this week."
  • ✓AI Brain, Human Filter: We build your private data warehouse, then deploy AI agents trained on your business to analyze 24/7.
  • ✓Fixed price, no drama: Productized tiers. No hourly billing. 48-hour onboarding.

09 Operations Workflow - The Monday Memo

FRI 16:00 PHT → n8n triggers full sync (Shopify, Ads, GA4)
FRI 18:00 → dbt runs models, updates BigQuery tables
SAT 09:00 → LangChain agent runs 7 analysis prompts:
1. week_over_week_revenue_change 2. ad_waste_detection 3. inventory_risk
4. top_losing_skus 5. cohort_retention 6. forecast_next_14d 7. anomaly_check
SAT 14:00 → Claude 3.5 drafts memo in Notion (human review queue)
SUN 19:00 → You review 30 min, record 5-min Loom
MON 08:00 CLIENT TIME → Slack: Memo + Loom + 3 decisions

Each memo follows template: TL;DR (3 bullets) → What Changed → 3 Decisions → Metrics Table → Risks. Average production time per client after Month 3: 1.5 hours/week.
